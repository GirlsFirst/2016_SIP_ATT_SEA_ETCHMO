This robot was originally made as a Girls Who Code final project, started in August of 2016. The Etch-Mo robot named LC began as a device used just to amuse children then it evoloved into a robot that would help ease a child's hospital aniexty.


Today Etch-Mo has hardware and software limitations, but our mission for the future is to better the techology so that the capablity of Etch-Mo will expand and become a tool as well as a companion for children to play with.
