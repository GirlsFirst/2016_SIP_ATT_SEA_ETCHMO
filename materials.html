For this project we had used a lot of different materials. Most of what you see on the robot is extra things that were need to be ordered online via Amazon. The budget that we had was of $100 that Girls Who Code was providing.


This the list of items that Girls Who Code provided:
  • Two Ardino Boards
  • Two Circuit Boards
  • A LOT OF WIRES
  • Two breadboards
  • Multiple Sharpies
  • LOTS OF TAPE
  • Pieces of cardboard
  • Wheels
  • Chop-Sticks
  • Metal Structure of Robot

This is the list of items that were needed to be bought or borrowed:
  • Four motion sensors
  • Two servos (used to make the pen)
  • Jumper cables
  • Cables
